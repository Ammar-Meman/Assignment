Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a vibrant blend of history and modernity. Known for its rich cultural heritage, Delhi houses iconic landmarks such as the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate. Walking through its streets is like taking a journey through time, from ancient Mughal architecture to bustling markets.

The city is famous for its diverse cuisine — from spicy street foods like chaat and golgappas to world-class fine dining restaurants. Delhi is also a paradise for shoppers, with places like Chandni Chowk, Connaught Place, and Dilli Haat offering everything from traditional handicrafts to modern brands.

Beyond its monuments and markets, Delhi is also the political and cultural heart of India. It hosts major festivals, concerts, and events that attract people from all over the country. Visiting Delhi is not just a trip — it's an experience that captures the essence of India.
